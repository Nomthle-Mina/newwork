About

Hello! I'm Nomthle Mina, a passionate vegan and web developer. My journey to veganism began 2 years ago, when I started learning about the environmental and animal welfare impacts of the food industry. As I delved deeper into the world of plant-based living, I discovered the numerous health benefits and felt inspired to make a positive change in my life.

Welcome to my vegan page! The purpose of this page is to share my passion for veganism, provide valuable resources and information, and connect with like-minded individuals. On this page, you can expect to find: Delicious vegan recipes and cooking tips, Product reviews and recommendations for vegan-friendly food, clothing, and lifestyle products, Personal stories and experiences from my own vegan journey.
